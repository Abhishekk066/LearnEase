Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up for a course?
Signing up for a course is easy! Browse our course catalog, select the course you're interested in, and click "Enroll Now." If you don't have an account yet, you'll be prompted to create one before completing your enrollment.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. For corporate or bulk enrollments, we also offer invoice-based payments.
Can I access courses on mobile devices?
Yes! LearnEase is fully responsive and works great on smartphones and tablets. You can access your courses anytime, anywhere, from any device with an internet connection.
Do you offer refunds if I'm not satisfied?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for most of our courses. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can request a refund within 30 days of enrollment.
How long do I have access to a course after purchasing?
Once you purchase a course, you have lifetime access to it! You can revisit the content whenever you need a refresher or want to continue your learning journey.
